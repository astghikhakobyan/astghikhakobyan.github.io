Biography

I am a research scientist at the Center for Scientific Innovations and Education (CSIE) and an assistant professor at the National Polytechnic University of Armenia (NPUA), based at the Aerial Robotics Research and Education Center of NPUA in Yerevan. I earned my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Seoul National University (SNU), Seoul, under the guidance of Professor Insoon Yang.

My academic journey began with a B.S. degree in Automation and Control from the NPUA, Yerevan, in 2018. I further pursued my passion by attaining an M.S. degree in ECE from SNU in 2020. During my time at SNU, I was honored with the Distinguished ECE M.S. Dissertation Award and the Distinguished ECE Ph.D. Dissertation Award, recognizing my dedication and excellence in the field.

My research interests encompass control and optimization, motion planning, and the development of safe autonomous systems. I am deeply committed to advancing these areas of study and contributing meaningfully to the field of scientific innovations and education.

I am also a recipient of the "Incoming Research Fellowship Program 2024 (Integration Grant)” by the Higher Education and Science Committee of the RA MoESCS.
