Biography

I am a Research Scientist at the Center for Scientific Innovations and Education (CSIE), based at the Aerial Robotics Education and Research Center of the National Polytechnic University of Armenia (NPUA) in Yerevan. I earned my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Seoul National University (SNU), Seoul, under the guidance of Professor Insoon Yang.

My academic journey began with a B.S. degree in Automation and Control from the National Polytechnic University of Armenia (NPUA), Yerevan, in 2018. I further pursued my passion by attaining an M.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from SNU in 2020. During my time at SNU, I was honored with the Distinguished ECE M.S. Dissertation Award and the Distinguished ECE Ph.D. Dissertation Award, recognizing my dedication and excellence in the field.

My research interests encompass control and optimization, motion planning, and the development of safe autonomous systems. I am deeply committed to advancing these areas of study and contributing meaningfully to the field of scientific innovations and education.
